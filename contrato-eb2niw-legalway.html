Purpose of the Agreement

The Contractor shall provide exclusively administrative support, document preparation, evidence organization, case assembly coordination, business plan preparation when included, and operational assistance related to the preparation of an employment-based immigration petition, including EB2-NIW (Employment-Based Second Preference — National Interest Waiver) when applicable, according to the scope, pricing, timelines, and specifications outlined in the approved commercial proposal.

The Contractor does not provide legal services, legal advice, legal opinions, or legal representation. The Contractor is not a law firm and does not represent the Client before USCIS or any governmental agency.

Included Services (only when expressly described in the approved commercial proposal):

  • Initial administrative profile review for document preparation purposes
  • Resume/CV review and organization
  • Evidence checklist and document organization
  • Review and organization of supporting documentation
  • Guidance regarding the administrative format and collection of recommendation letters
  • Petition package organization and administrative case support
  • Business plan preparation, when included in the approved commercial proposal
  • Case assembly coordination and shipping coordination, only when expressly included
  • Administrative support for Requests for Evidence (RFEs), subject to Section 4.3

Excluded Services — Unless specifically agreed in writing, this Agreement DOES NOT include:

  • USCIS filing fees; Premium Processing fees; Attorney fees
  • Legal representation before USCIS or government agencies; legal advice; legal opinions
  • Publication or media placement services; patent filing services; expert opinion acquisition
  • Credential evaluations; translation services; government fees; appeals; litigation
  • Motions to reopen or reconsider; academic publication production; third-party reports
  • Any other service not expressly included in the approved commercial proposal

These expenses remain solely the responsibility of the Client.

1.2 Immediate Start of Services: By signing this Agreement, approving the commercial proposal, authorizing payment, or making the first payment, whichever occurs first, the Client authorizes immediate commencement of services.

2.5 Information Updates

04
EB-2/NIW Specific Terms & Limitation of Liability

4.1 No Approval Guarantee. The Client acknowledges that approval of any employment-based immigration petition, including EB2-NIW, is entirely discretionary and determined solely by USCIS. No approval, immigration benefit, visa issuance, green card, priority date movement, processing timeline, or government outcome is guaranteed. The Contractor's total liability shall not exceed the amount actually paid by the Client.

4.3 RFE Response Included Under Same Scope

If USCIS issues a Request for Evidence related to the petition prepared by the Contractor, the Contractor shall provide administrative support for the RFE response at no additional service fee, provided that: the RFE relates to the same petition; the response does not require a new case structure, new professional profile, new proposed endeavor, or new strategy; and the Client has paid all amounts due and provides all requested documents within required timeframes.

4.3.4 Refiling After Denial

If USCIS denies the petition, the Contractor shall not charge an additional service fee for administrative support for one (1) refiling of the same petition, provided that the refiling is based on substantially the same professional profile and case content, the denial was not caused by false or omitted information from the Client, all amounts due are paid, and all updated documents are provided within required timeframes. The Client remains responsible for all USCIS filing fees, government fees, and third-party costs for the refiling.

4.3.6 No Guarantee After RFE or Refiling: RFE support or refiling support does not guarantee approval. All immigration decisions are made exclusively by USCIS or the applicable government authority.

4.4 Timeline Disclaimer: Preparation timelines depend on Client responsiveness, documentation complexity, third-party participation, payment status, and changes requested by the Client. Specific filing dates are not guaranteed.

4.7 Legal Representation Disclaimer

"I AM NOT AN ATTORNEY LICENSED TO PRACTICE LAW AND MAY NOT PROVIDE LEGAL ADVICE OR LEGAL REPRESENTATION." The Contractor does not represent clients before USCIS, does not provide legal advice or issue legal opinions, does not act as an attorney, and does not establish an attorney-client relationship. If legal representation becomes necessary, the Client must independently retain licensed legal counsel.
